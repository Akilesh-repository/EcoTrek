Our Mission

Bridging the gap between tourism and restoration.

EcoTrek was founded on a simple principle: travel should leave a destination better than it was found. We partner with local NGOs, scientists, and indigenous communities to create volunteer programs that directly fund and support critical environmental initiatives worldwide.

  • Ethical Wildlife Encounters

    Strict no-touch policies ensuring animal welfare comes first.

  • Community Empowered

    100% of our ground staff are hired from local communities.

The Process

How to become an EcoTrek Volunteer.

We've streamlined the onboarding process to ensure you are fully prepared, trained, and equipped for your conservation journey before you even pack your bags.

1

Apply & Match

Fill out our detailed application. Our coordination team will match your skills, physical fitness level, and interests with a critical conservation project that needs your specific help.

2

Virtual Training Camp

Gain access to the EcoTrek portal. Complete comprehensive digital modules covering local ecology, cultural sensitivities, basic language skills, and field safety protocols.

3

Deploy & Conserve

Arrive at basecamp where you'll be greeted by local guides. Integrate with your volunteer team and spend the next few weeks engaging in hands-on, high-impact conservation work.
